What's my game

I believe in bioremediation's potential to solve humanity's woes. I come from a place where the progress of humanity has left a devastating mark on many people's quality of life through pollution of air, water, and land. I believe that design has the power to not only influence ways of thinking, but also of reshaping our methods. We can mimic the most important aspects of nature, such as its ability to reuse all of its byproducts into new life.
